Multiple Disease Prediction

Heart Disease

CAD happens when coronary arteries struggle to supply the heart with enough blood, oxygen and nutrients. Cholesterol deposits, or plaques, narrow your arteries, decreasing blood flow to your heart, causing chest pain, shortness of breath or heart attack.

Diabetes

Diabetes is a chronic disease that occurs when the pancreas does not produce enough insulin or when the body cannot effectively use the insulin it produces. Insulin regulates blood glucose. If left untreated, it leads to serious complications.

Parkinson's Disease

Parkinson's disease is a progressive nervous system disorder that affects movement. It starts with a tremor in one hand and develops into stiffness, slow movement, and impaired balance. Early intervention can help manage symptoms.
